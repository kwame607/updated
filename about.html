The story

From Kumasi,
to your doorstep.

WiseWave Enterprise was born in Kumasi, Ashanti Region with a simple but powerful idea: people in Ghana deserve a safe, transparent, and convenient way to buy premium electronics and vehicles — without the anxiety of sending money to unknown social media accounts.

Founded by Gabriel Antwi Osei and officially registered with the Registrar of Business Names on 13 February 2024, WiseWave started as a small operation and quickly grew into a trusted name in the Kumasi tech and auto market.

We deliberately focus on three product lines — gaming consoles, iPhones, and premium cars — because we believe in doing fewer things extraordinarily well. Every product we stock is verified, every price is transparent, and every payment is secured through Paystack.

"We wanted to build the kind of business we wished existed when we were the customer — one that actually picks up the phone, delivers what it promises, and makes the whole experience feel premium."

— Gabriel Antwi Osei, Founder & Proprietor

Today WiseWave serves customers across Ghana — from Kumasi and Accra to Takoradi and beyond — with plans to expand the product range and open a physical showroom in the Ashanti Region.

Our journey

How we got here.

January 2024
WiseWave Enterprise founded
Gabriel Antwi Osei starts WiseWave Enterprise in Kumasi, beginning operations on 31 January 2024 with a focus on automobile imports and mobile money services.
February 2024
Officially registered
WiseWave Enterprise is officially registered under the Registration of Business Names Act, 1962 (No.151) as Reg. No. BN137080224.
Mid 2024
Product range expanded
WiseWave adds gaming consoles and iPhones to the catalogue, creating a focused three-category offering built around what Ghanaian customers demand most.
2025
Official e-commerce website launched
WiseWave launches its full online store with Paystack integration, allowing customers to browse, order, and pay securely without sending DMs.
