Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

How do I pay with MoMo?+
At checkout, enter your details and click "Place Order & Pay with MoMo." A payment prompt will be sent to your phone number. Enter your MoMo PIN to approve. You'll receive an instant email and SMS confirmation.
Are the products genuine / authentic?+
Yes. Every product we sell is 100% authentic and comes in original sealed packaging. We do not sell clones, refurbished or open-box items unless clearly stated in the product description.
How long does delivery take?+
Within Kumasi: same day (orders before 12pm). Accra: 1–2 business days. Other regions: 3–5 business days. You'll receive tracking updates via SMS and email.
Can I return a product?+
Yes. We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is in its original condition and sealed packaging. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to initiate a return.
Can I view a car before buying?+
Yes. For vehicles, we highly encourage you to arrange a physical viewing before payment. Contact us on 0595 650 930 to schedule an appointment at our Kumasi location.
Is WiseWave a registered business?+
Yes. WiseWave Enterprise is officially registered under the Registration of Business Names Act, 1962 (No.151), Republic of Ghana. Registration No. BN137080224, TIN: P0020513828. You can verify this with the Registrar General's Department.
